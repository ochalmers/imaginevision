# Imagine — Full Site Map

**Version:** 1.0
**Total pages:** 36 (core) + blog/dynamic content

---

## URL Structure Conventions

- All URLs: lowercase, hyphen-separated
- No trailing slashes
- Hub pages act as both navigation landing pages and standalone pages
- Blog, guides, and case studies are content collections with individual item pages

---

## Top-Level Navigation

```
/ (homepage)
/platform
/solutions
/industries
/developers
/resources
/company
```
