# Page Spec: /case-studies

## Page Objective
Provide commercial evidence that Imagine delivers measurable outcomes. Give prospective customers proof that brands like theirs have built on Imagine and seen real results — in the specific metrics that matter to ecommerce and operations leaders.

## Audience
- Ecommerce directors and digital product leads evaluating Imagine for their brand
- Commercial and strategy leads making or influencing platform investment decisions
- Technical leads who need customer evidence to support an internal recommendation
- Investors and analysts building a picture of Imagine's commercial traction

## Key Message
Brands that build on Imagine see measurable commercial outcomes — fewer returns, higher conversion, faster variant launches, and product lines that previously could not exist online. The evidence is specific, attributed, and verifiable.

## Copy Notes
- Every stat must be attributable. Use "[Brand descriptor]" placeholders until brands confirm public attribution.
- The "Outcome" field on each card should be a single, specific number — not a vague description.
- The aggregate proof bar should include the methodology note to maintain credibility.
- Pull quotes should sound like real people said them, not like marketing copy.

## Design Notes
- Featured case study at full width with strong visual hierarchy — headline stat should be largest text element
- Case study index in a 2-column grid on desktop
- Outcome stat on each card in a larger, bolder type size than surrounding text
- Aggregate proof bar as a high-contrast band separating index from CTA section
- Industry tags as colour-coded chips for scannability
