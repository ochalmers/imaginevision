# Page Spec: /solutions

## Meta
- **URL:** /solutions
- **Title:** Solutions — Imagine
- **Description:** Imagine powers every layer of configurable commerce — from buyer-facing configurators to automated marketing assets and product design workflows.
- **Audience:** Company buyers (heads of ecommerce, product, marketing, creative operations at furniture, mattress, appliance, and lighting brands)
- **Page objective:** Orient visitors to Imagine's four solution areas and route them to the right sub-page. Establish that Imagine is a platform — not a point tool.

---

## Key Message

**"The right solution for every configurable commerce problem."**

Configurable products create complexity at every layer: the product page, the marketing calendar, the design studio, the sales floor. Imagine resolves that complexity — with a single platform that powers the full lifecycle of a configurable product.

---

## Tone & Design Direction

- Minimal grid layout. Four solution tiles dominate the page.
- Each tile feels like a distinct product — not a feature list.
- Typography-led. No decorative illustration. Product renders used contextually.
- Stripe-style routing page: clear hierarchy, confident copy, fast path to detail.
