# Page Spec — /contact

**Page objective:** Route inbound enquiries to the right team. Convert interested visitors into qualified conversations.
**Primary audience:** All
**Primary CTA:** Send message
**Key message:** Let's talk.

---

## Page Objective

Contact is a conversion and routing page. It should feel premium and direct — not a form wall. The goal is to make the right person feel comfortable starting a conversation.

---

## Section Flow

### Hero
**Heading:** Let's talk.
**Sub:** Whether you're exploring configurable commerce for your brand, building on the platform, or looking to partner — we want to hear from you.

### Contact Options (3 columns)

**Column 1: For companies**
Label: Sales enquiry
Text: Interested in Imagine for your products? Talk to our team about what configurable commerce looks like for your brand.
CTA: `Request a demo`
Link: → routes to demo request form

**Column 2: For developers**
Label: Developer support
Text: Technical questions about the API, SDK, or platform? Reach our developer support team.
CTA: `developer-support@imagine.com`
Link: → email link + link to /developers/docs

**Column 3: Other**
Label: Press, partnerships, other
Text: For media enquiries, partnership discussions, or anything else.
CTA: `hello@imagine.com`

### Contact Form (below columns)
A clean single-column form for general enquiries:
- Name (text)
- Company (text)
- Email (email)
- Enquiry type (select: Sales / Developer / Partnership / Press / Other)
- Message (textarea)
- Submit CTA: `Send message`

### Location / Social (footer of page)
- Office location(s) if applicable
- LinkedIn, GitHub links

---

## Design Notes
- Form should feel minimal — not like a lead capture trap
- No "required" field asterisks — keep it clean, validate on submit
- Confirmation state: replace form with a clean "Thanks — we'll be in touch." message
