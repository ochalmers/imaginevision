# Page Spec: /industries

## Meta
- **URL:** /industries
- **Title:** Industries — Imagine
- **Description:** Imagine is purpose-built for the industries that sell configurable physical products — furniture, mattresses, appliances, and lighting. Vertical depth, not generic tooling.
- **Audience:** Company buyers across furniture, mattress, appliance, and lighting brands — including ecommerce leads, CTOs, heads of product, and brand directors
- **Page objective:** Signal that Imagine has genuine vertical depth — not generic visualisation software stretched to fit multiple categories. Route visitors to the right industry page and create confidence that Imagine understands their specific problems.

---

## Key Message

**"Built for the industries that configure."**

Configurable commerce is not a horizontal problem. A furniture brand's challenges — enormous SKU counts, lifestyle photography at scale, B2B catalogue complexity — are not the same as a mattress brand's challenges around guided selling and return reduction. Imagine is built for both. And for appliances. And for lighting.

---

## Tone & Design Direction

- Parallel structure with the /solutions hub — same grid treatment, different axis.
- Each industry card should feel immediately specific. No category platitudes.
- Show industry-relevant product renders in each tile — not icons, not abstract shapes.
- Stripe/Linear: confident routing page. Gets out of the way fast.
