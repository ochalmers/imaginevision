### 3. How It Works

**Heading:** From brand brief to asset pipeline.

**Steps:**

1. **Submit your brief** — Scene direction, lighting mood, product range, output formats, and brand guidelines. Our team (or your team via the API) translates this into render parameters.
2. **Imagine generates the assets** — Our pipeline renders every requested product configuration in the specified scene and format. You're not selecting from templates — you're getting purpose-built creative.
3. **Review and deliver** — Assets are delivered to your DAM, shared via a review link, or pushed directly to your platform. Revisions are render changes, not reshoot requests.

**Design notes:** Three steps with a visual showing the brief (a structured input form or doc), the pipeline (a render grid in progress), and the output (a finished campaign image). Clean horizontal flow.
